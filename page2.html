Emissaries are a way for pirates to represent one of the five trading companys. Sailing as an emissary will provide your crew with many benefits like higher payouts and reputation gains and specific treasure items for the company that your crew is representing. The companys that you can represent are the Gold Hoarders, Order of Souls, the Merchant Alliance, Reaper's Bones, and Athena's Fortune. To represent a company one of your crew mates must buy the emissary flag of that company.
