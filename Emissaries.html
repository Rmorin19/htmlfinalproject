Emissaries are a way for pirates to represent one of the five trading companys. Sailing as an emissary will provide your crew with many benefits like higher payouts and reputation gains and specific treasure items for the company that your crew is representing. The companys that you can represent are the Gold Hoarders, Order of Souls, the Merchant Alliance, Reaper's Bones, and Athena's Fortune. To represent a company one of your crew mates must buy the emissary flag of that company. Each flag cost 20000 gold. Emissaries have 5 grades that will give a higher bonus for each grade as well as a higher flag retrieval bonus. Each company will have a emissary quest that will be unlocked at grade V.

Gold Hoarders

The Gold Hoarders emissary flag can be bought from any gold hoarder at the outposts. The Gold Hoarders emissary quest can be bought from any gold hoarder once the crew has reached emissary grade 5. To get to higher grades you must bring treasure chests to the gold hoarders. Unfortuantly only one emissary quest may be bought per emissary flag. The Gold Hoarders quest gives the crew 4 treasure maps with 5 x's each. The chest's unearthed will always be Captain's chests.


Order of Souls

The Order of Souls emissary much like the Gold Hoarders emissary requires you to bring treasure to rank up but to them tresure is skeleton sculls. To get these sculls you must fight waves of skeletons in the strongholds. Another thing you can do is fight skeleton captains that can be found on many islands through out the ocean. The skeleton captains will drop sculls aswell. This emissary quest gives the crew bounty maps with skeleton captains that will each drop a scull.


Merchant Alliance

The Merchant Alliance's way of ranking up is for you to give them Crates, Animals, Gunpower Barrels or Mermaid Gems. You can find these things on most islands and you should not find much trouble getting them. This quest will have your crew recive to maps with 10 crates that must be delivered to other merchants in 30 in game days.


Reper's Bones

The Repers Bones emissary will grant you the ability to rank up after you returne with the emissary flags of others aswell as treasure. To get the flags you must sink there ships. The flag can only be bought at Reapers Hideout from the servant of the flame. Once you have reached grade V you will be able to see other ships on your map making it easier to find and sink other ships. Unfortuantly this emissary doesn't have a quest and the only place to give back the flags and treasure is Reapers Hideout it is a more difficult emissary.

Athena's Fortune

The Athena's Fortune emissary is only avalible once you have be acknowledged as a pirate lord. You can get the flag at Athena's Hideout or from mysterious srangers in taverns. The quest wil give your crew a treasure map with 7 x's, a riddle map that finds a collectors chest and a bounty map with 4 skeleton captains to kill. Once those tasks have been completed you will recive a treasure map with 2 x's with one unearthing a chest of legends and the other a keg of ancient black powder.
